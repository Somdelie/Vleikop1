About Us

Vleikop Chartered Accountant . (“VCA”) is an indigenous black firm founded in the Republic of South Africa. Our business activities are focused on auditing, accounting, advisory and taxation services and are not restricted in nature. VCA is a firm of Chartered Accountants that provides technical advisory services to audit firms and companies. These services include tax opinions, IFRS opinions as well as in-house seminars. We are a young company looking to push the boundaries of excellence and committed to delivering exceptional service to its clients. Due to the size of our company, every client gets special attention and is treated as if it is the only client. We are also a certified level 1 BBBEE contributor and 100% Black owned and managed. VCA provides innovative solutions to both public and private sector clients including those listed on the Johannesburg Stock Exchange.

Our Philosophy

VCA sets the benchmark in integrity, equality and professionalism; and is driven to inspire new standards of excellence. A rich heritage combined with robust leadership guides the firm in its vision, mission, values and philosophy. Part of VCA’s commitment to our staff is to equip them with the necessary tools (i.e. up-to-date technical knowledge, knowhow and best practice information) to exceed client expectations. Training and development are crucial components of the firm's employee practices and represents our continued investment in building corporate citizenship. The proven capabilities and strong focus on service delivery of VCA undoubtedly poises the firm for greater growth and industry prominence.

To offer innovative, efficient, flexible and highly professional and competitive assurance and advisory solutions through:
• ensuring client’s needs are a company priority;
• producing quality and excellent assurance and advisory work;
• high level of staff competence and commitment;
• strict compliance with professional standards

VCA strives to create a service culture that permeates the entire firm. We aim to contribute towards the growth and development of our clients, staff and country, and consequently our own growth. We will ensure that the firm represents unity and a shared vision among management and staff in line with the company's pillars of client and people centricity.

We intend to enhance transparency, accountability and proper compliance by emerging and growing businesses and government institutions.
