Company Secretarial

Outsourcing your company secretarial duties to our experienced staff will ensure compliance with required legislation, giving you added valuable time to focus on your business. Getting started is as simple as getting in touch and telling us about your requirements.
We help our clients stay on top of their interests by keeping abreast of changes in the industry, using highly trained and vastly experienced staff as well as maintaining impressive turnaround times.

Full range of services includes:

  • Formation and registration of companies and trusts
  • Registrations for VAT, PAYE, UIF and Workmen’s Compensation
  • Trust statutory administration
  • Company and CC name changes and reservations
  • Change of Shareholders and/or Members
  • Preparation and submission of statutory documentation to the CIPC
  • Sale of shelf companies
  • Preparation of other Minutes and notices
  • Conversion of the status of a company, as well as deregistration with the CIPC
  • Statutory Audits
  • Preparation and submission of CIPC Annual Returns
  • Drafting of business plan
  • Change of financial year ends
  • Alterations to Memorandum of Incorporation
